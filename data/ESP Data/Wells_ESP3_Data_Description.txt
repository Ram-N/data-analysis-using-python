
DATA DESCRIPTION

File: ESP_Wells3.csv

This file contains the Operating characteristics of >1000 ESP's (Submersible pumps)
that operate on wells in a large field. The data contains values take during *SPOT Inspections.*

The ESP's come in 3 sizes - 4.5, 5.5 and 7 inches.

The intake readings (Pressure, Temperature) and motor readings (Oil temperature, vibration),
current Leakage (mA) and the ROC Pressure.

The surface temperature of the well is provided, as well as the TDH readings.

Finally, the overall condtion of the ESP based on the inspection is noted 
as Normal or Failed
